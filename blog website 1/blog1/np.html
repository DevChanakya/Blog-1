Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open a savings account?

To open a savings account, you can visit our nearest branch or go to our online portal. Follow the simple application process, provide the required documents, and your account will be opened.

What is the minimum balance for a checking account?

The minimum balance requirement for a checking account varies depending on the type of account you choose. Contact our customer support for specific account details.

How can I apply for a credit card?

You can apply for a credit card online through our website or by visiting our branch. You'll need to provide personal and financial information, and your application will be reviewed by our team.

What are the interest rates on your savings accounts?

Interest rates on savings accounts can vary and may change over time. Visit our Rates & Fees page for the latest information on interest rates.
